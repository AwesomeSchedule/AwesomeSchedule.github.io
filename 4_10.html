Weekly Activity April 10th

Project Activity

This past week included a lot of working together to get everything to come together. We collaborated in working through some communication issues that arose between the front and back-end of our project in addition to knocking out a solid testing plan. We want to make certain that we have provided our client with the best possible application we can, so we were thorough in discussing what is reasonable for us to complete in the weeks to come. Although there are still some hurdles that we have left to overcome, we feel confident that we will be able to finish strong.

Michael Ho

Progress had its momentum stopped when some issues arose related to the communication between the Ember Front-End and the Server-side API. I have worked closely with Joey this weekend to help iron them out as much as they can. As it is integral to get that squared away, that will be our full focus for the days to come. The sooner we can get that worked out, the sooner we can start working on the other features. One thing that I will intend to look at is Ember.JS's preference for a JSON format in the response it gets from the server. Getting that perfected will make it easier for us in the long run.
